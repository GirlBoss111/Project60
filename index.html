Why Are Sports So Important?

Sports are, first of all, really, REALLY cool. Organized, well-structured youth sports and on-going physical activities can provide many benefits for children and adolescents. Physical exercise is good for the mind, body and spirit. Team sports help teach adolescents accountability, dedication, leadership and other skills.
